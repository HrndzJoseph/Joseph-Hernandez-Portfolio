MiniVille

We’ve created a small digital game inspired by the board game Minivilles.
Developed in Unity with a group of friends, our game aims to capture the essence of building a city from scratch, similar to Minivilles.
It’s a modest project that we’re proud of and we hope players will enjoy the unique experience it offers.
